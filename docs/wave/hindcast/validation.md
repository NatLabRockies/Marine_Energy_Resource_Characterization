# Validation

## Validation Summary

The WaveWatch III and SWAN models were extensively validated against observational data including:

- Common wave parameters (significant wave height, peak period, mean direction)
- Six IEC resource parameters
- 2D wave spectra from high-quality spectral buoy data

Detailed validation results are published in the references below.

## West Coast

Wu et al. (2020) developed and validated the high-resolution regional wave hindcast model for the U.S. West Coast, demonstrating good agreement with buoy observations across multiple wave parameters.

Yang et al. (2020) characterized the nearshore wave resource variability along the U.S. West Coast.

## Atlantic

Allahdadi et al. (2019a) developed and validated the regional-scale high-resolution unstructured model for the U.S. East Coast. Allahdadi et al. (2019b) investigated sensitivity to whitecapping parameterizations.

Ahn et al. (2021) performed nearshore wave energy resource characterization along the East Coast.
